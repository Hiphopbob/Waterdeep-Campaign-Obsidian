Not many people still exist that know of Walter’s early years. And fewer people still are even interested in looking into the background of a “mere butler”, even one that serves the noble Margaster family. 

Even the Margasters only see a slightly aged elf, not worth their time or thought, as long as they are served adequately. If you decide to look very close you might spot the tiniest sign of a wrinkle or a single light gray hair. The only people showing him any additional attention is the current matriarch and her granddaughter, Miss [[Ilddrith Solara Margaster]]. 

Since the early childhood of Miss Margaster, Walter has been her personal butler and teacher. He was specifically assigned shortly after The Accident, and tasked with providing any guidance and assistance possible to the young girl.

Despite his calm and collected behaviour, almost cold and robotic to such a young girl, she soon warmed up to him, as he appeared as a constant in an otherwise chaotic and turbulent childhood. 

As the years passed, her powers grew stronger. Soon she would be ready, and his task would be complete. But it was only during the Initiation, that Walter noticed he had changed as well. He had to do something.

No one had even noticed him, until he was right next to Miss Margaster - and just as quickly, both of them had left the room, leaving everyone in confusion. Knowing every nook and cranny of the mansion, he quickly navigated them out into the street and hid from the pursuers.

The only two things that he is still sure of is the universal truth - there is strength in numbers and power through money. And they would need all the strength and power they could to survive.
